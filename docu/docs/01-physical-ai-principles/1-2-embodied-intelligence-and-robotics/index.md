### Learning Objectives:
*   Explain the concept of embodied intelligence and its theoretical underpinnings, particularly how physical interaction shapes cognitive processes.
*   Analyze how different robotic designs and sensorimotor capabilities contribute to the manifestation of embodied intelligence.
*   Evaluate various learning paradigms, such as reinforcement learning and developmental robotics, for enabling adaptation in embodied systems.
*   Discuss the principles and challenges of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), including safety, trust, and communication.
*   Examine contemporary case studies of embodied robotics, identifying their unique features and contributions to the field.
