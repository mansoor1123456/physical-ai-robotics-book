# High-Level Module Outlines: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

## Module 1: Physical AI Principles

**Learning Outcomes**: Understand the foundational concepts of physical AI, its ethical implications, and its application in robotics.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 1.1: Introduction to Physical AI
- Chapter 1.2: Embodied Intelligence and Robotics
- Chapter 1.3: Ethical Considerations in Physical AI

## Module 2: ROS 2 Foundations (Robotic Nervous System)

**Learning Outcomes**: Master the fundamentals of ROS 2 for robot communication, control, and data management.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 2.1: ROS 2 Architecture and Core Concepts
- Chapter 2.2: ROS 2 Nodes, Topics, and Services
- Chapter 2.3: ROS 2 Actions and Launch Files
- Chapter 2.4: Robot State Representation and URDF
- Chapter 2.5: ROS 2 Navigation Stack Overview

## Module 3: Gazebo Digital Twin (Simulation Environment)

**Learning Outcomes**: Develop skills in creating and simulating robot environments using Gazebo for digital twin development.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 3.1: Introduction to Gazebo Simulation
- Chapter 3.2: Creating Robot Models (SDF)
- Chapter 3.3: World Building and Environment Interaction
- Chapter 3.4: Integrating ROS 2 with Gazebo
- Chapter 3.5: Advanced Gazebo Simulation Techniques

## Module 4: Unity Simulation Pipeline

**Learning Outcomes**: Utilize Unity for advanced robot simulation, sensor integration, and reinforcement learning environments.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 4.1: Introduction to Unity for Robotics
- Chapter 4.2: Importing Robot Models and Assets
- Chapter 4.3: Sensor Simulation and Data Generation
- Chapter 4.4: Reinforcement Learning with Unity ML-Agents
- Chapter 4.5: ROS 2 - Unity Integration

## Module 5: NVIDIA Isaac AI Robot Brain

**Learning Outcomes**: Implement AI functionalities for robots using NVIDIA Isaac SDK, focusing on perception, navigation, and manipulation.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 5.1: Introduction to NVIDIA Isaac SDK
- Chapter 5.2: Isaac ROS and Perception Pipelines
- Chapter 5.3: Isaac Sim for Robot Development
- Chapter 5.4: Navigation and Path Planning with Isaac
- Chapter 5.5: Robot Manipulation and Grasping with Isaac

## Module 6: VLA (Voice, Language, Action) Robotics

**Learning Outcomes**: Integrate vision and language models with robotic systems to enable advanced human-robot interaction and task execution.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 6.1: Foundations of Vision-Language Models (VLMs)
- Chapter 6.2: Natural Language Understanding for Robotics
- Chapter 6.3: Speech Recognition and Synthesis for Robots
- Chapter 6.4: Action Grounding and Task Planning from Language
- Chapter 6.5: Human-Robot Collaboration via VLA

## Module 7: Humanoid Mechatronics Overview

**Learning Outcomes**: Gain an understanding of humanoid robot design, actuation, sensing, and control systems.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 7.1: Humanoid Robot Kinematics and Dynamics
- Chapter 7.2: Actuation Systems for Humanoids
- Chapter 7.3: Sensor Systems in Humanoid Robots
- Chapter 7.4: Balance and Whole-Body Control
- Chapter 7.5: Human-Inspired Motion Generation

## Module 8: Capstone Project: Autonomous Humanoid

**Learning Outcomes**: Apply all learned concepts to design, simulate, and potentially implement an autonomous humanoid robot solution for a complex task.

### Chapters:
- Chapter 8.1: Capstone Project Introduction and Requirements
- Chapter 8.2: Project Planning and Design
- Chapter 8.3: Implementation and Integration
- Chapter 8.4: Testing, Evaluation, and Debugging
- Chapter 8.5: Project Presentation and Documentation
